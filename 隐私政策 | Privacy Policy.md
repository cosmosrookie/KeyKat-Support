# KeyKat / 打字喵 隐私政策 | Privacy Policy

## 简体中文
**生效日期：2026-02-14**

- 我们不收集任何个人数据  
- 我们不进行任何形式的跟踪或画像  
- 应用不联网，不向任何第三方传输数据  
- 所有统计均在本地设备内完成（例如每日击键次数），且不包含按键内容  
- **权限使用说明：** 为统计击键次数，打字喵需要启用“辅助功能”权限。该权限仅用于在本地检测按键事件的发生，不会读取、记录或传输按键内容  
- 儿童与未成年人：本应用不面向特定人群，也不收集数据  
- 政策更新：如本政策发生变更，我们将在应用或官方网站上公布更新版本  
- 联系方式：请填写邮箱或网站

## English
**Effective Date: 2026-02-14**

- We do not collect any personal data  
- We do not perform tracking or profiling of users  
- The app does not connect to the internet; no data is transmitted to any third party  
- All statistics are processed locally on your device (e.g., daily keystroke count) and never include the content of your keystrokes  
- **Permission Use:** To count keystrokes, KeyKat requires the “Accessibility” permission. This permission is used solely to detect that a keystroke occurred on-device and does not read, record, or transmit the content of any keystrokes  
- Children: The app is not targeted at any specific group and collects no data  
- Policy Updates: If this policy changes, we will publish the updated version in the app or on the official website  
- Contact: please provide your email or website
